Fat Controller (Without Service-Repository Pattern)
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

A fat controller in ASP.NET Core is an anti-pattern where a controller class becomes overly complex and handles too many responsibilities beyond managing HTTP requests.

This typically includes incorporating business logic, data access operations, and complex validation directly within the controller's action methods.
